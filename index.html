// About us

Module Tech UK

Module Tech was founded on a simple combination that's rare in this trade: deep expertise in computing and circuit boards, paired with real hands-on garage experience. That means we don't just plug in a tool and hope — we understand the electronics at component level and the vehicles they sit in.

From performance remapping to board-level repair, module recovery and cloning, we work across cars, vans, trucks, agricultural and plant machinery. Whether it's a faulty ECU, a corrupted module or a remap for more performance and economy, the work is done with genuine technical know-how.

Our focus is straightforward: precise work, honest pricing and getting your vehicle running as it should — without the cost and delay of main-dealer replacement.

// Questions

Frequently asked

What vehicles do you work on?

Cars, vans, trucks, agricultural machinery and plant. If you're unsure whether yours is covered, get in touch and ask.

Do I have to bring the vehicle to you?

In many cases you can remove and post the module to us. For some work the vehicle may need to be present — we'll tell you which applies when you enquire.

What's the difference between repair, recovery and cloning?

Repair fixes a faulty module. Recovery rescues data from a damaged or corrupted unit. Cloning copies your existing data onto a replacement module, avoiding dealer reprogramming.

How much does it cost?

It depends on the vehicle, module and work involved. Send us the details and we'll give you a clear price before anything starts.

Is remapping safe for my vehicle?

Our remaps are calibrated to your specific vehicle rather than generic files, with drivability and reliability in mind. We're happy to talk through what's appropriate for yours.
